--------- WELCOME TO AYISA INVESTMENTS COMPANY LTD ---------

"it's the most trusted company in east africa."

The AYISA Tent Company is an African pearl of distinction. We are passionate about the quality and style of our unique South Sudan made canvas Tents and our aim is to provide excellence in both the quality of the tents and the service. As architecturally beautiful as they are robust, these spacious tents are constructed from strong all color canvas and can withstand even the blusters and vagaries of a of the African climate.

From initial contact to post deployment, we strive to please with our bespoke service - each and every service is treated with the importance it deserves. Although based in South Sudan, Africa, we are able to set up anywhere in the world, so don't let location restrict you - our team have experience erecting tents in the most extraordinary places.

The iconic Ayisa Tent design originates from the African architecture of the 13th century. The beautiful symmetry and clean lines of the Ayisa Tent is reminiscent of the great palaces of the African dynasties. The African dynasties themselves erected structures similar to these in huge quantities to create vast tented camps, in celebration of royal weddings and special events.

The Ayisa Tent's geometric shape and uniform design is also widely associated with Egyptian Art, an infinitely popular style evocative of 1700 egyptian designes. With this perfect union of African design and East African engineering, Ayisa Tents are the only African dynasties rememberances available to hire in Africa with South Sudan's engineered frames, and can withstand winds of up to 40mph.

Stunning all color canvas tents exteriors & rustic detailing make Ayisa Tents an extremely beautiful combination between contemporary features and traditional style
